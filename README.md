# Daily Schedule — Gmail Add-on

A Gmail add-on that emails you a summary of your Google Calendar events —
a daily view of tomorrow's events, and a full-week schedule on your chosen day.
Available in Hebrew and English.

## Privacy
The add-on only reads your own calendar and emails you. It never stores or shares your data.
