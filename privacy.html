Privacy Policy — Daily Calendar Email

Last updated: July 2026

Daily Calendar Email ("the add-on") sends you a daily or weekly summary of your own Google Calendar events by email. This policy explains how it handles your data.

What the add-on accesses

  • Your Google Calendar events — only to read them and build your schedule summary.
  • Your email address — only to send the summary to you.
  • Permission to send email on your behalf — only to send the summary to your own address.

How your data is used

Your calendar data is used only to generate the schedule email that is sent to you. The add-on does not store, save, share, sell, or transmit your data to anyone else. Nothing leaves your own Google account.

Data storage

The add-on saves only your own settings (preferred send time, timezone, language, and weekly-summary day) using Google's built-in per-user storage. It does not store your calendar events or personal data on any external server.

Third parties

The add-on does not use any third-party services and does not send your data to any third party.
