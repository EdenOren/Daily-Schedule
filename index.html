📅 Daily Calendar Email

A simple Gmail add-on that emails you a summary of your Google Calendar events — every day, and a full-week view on your chosen day. Available in Hebrew and English.

What it does

  • Sends a daily email with tomorrow's events.
  • Sends a full-week schedule on the day you pick (e.g. Saturday).
  • Choose your send time, timezone, and language.

Privacy

The add-on only reads your own calendar and emails you. It never stores or shares your data. See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
